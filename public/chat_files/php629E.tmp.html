Consulting Solutions

Accelerating marketing transformation with expert advice and support.

Collaboration and support models designed for a new marketing era.

Global data and activation platforms make it possible for marketing teams to manage more and more of their operations in-house. Essence helps companies take advantage of self-service platforms and strengthen their capabilities with custom consulting, enablement, and support services.

We help practitioners at all levels establish the appropriate talent, technology, and processes for their unique business needs. Access to Essence’s industry-leading expertise and tools helps client side marketers eliminate risk and increase the effectiveness and efficiency of their teams.

Making media work smarter.

01

Consulting

Essence provides consulting and auditing support for marketing teams in three phases: defining business goals, auditing and assessing organizational capacity and processes, and designing an appropriate model to meet the needs of the business.

02

Enabling

Our consulting offering makes Essence’s industry-leading expertise and support available on a customized basis. Services include recruitment, training and development, and technology specification and set up.

03

Sustaining

Essence helps companies sustain marketing operations with access to market intelligence, value and performance benchmarking, technology, and additional staff to support seasonal demand.
